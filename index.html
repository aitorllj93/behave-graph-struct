Customizable

While this library contains a lot of nodes, you do not have to expose all of them. You can also include your custom nodes.

High Performance

Currently in performance testing, the library achieves over 2M node executions per second.

Designed for Integration

This library is designed to be extended with context dependent nodes, specifically Actions, Events and Queries that match the capabilities and requirements of your system.
